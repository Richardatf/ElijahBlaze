Beneath Eden

Before Elijah Blaze, there was Elijah Blue.

Elijah's dreams begin as fragments: blue light under stone, corridors without doors, symbols he has never learned but somehow understands. Then the compass turns toward West Ridge. The earth answers. And far below the desert, something remembers him.

This is not the story of a boy who finds a machine. It is the story of two minds—one human, one architectural—learning whether they can trust each other.
